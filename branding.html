Not All Vinyl Is Created Equal.

Many shops use "Polymeric" vinyl which shrinks and peels after 12 months in the Australian sun.

We exclusively use Premium Cast Vinyls (Avery Dennison / 3M). Cast vinyl is poured as a liquid, meaning it has no "memory" to shrink back. It conforms to deep recesses and stays looking painted-on for years.
