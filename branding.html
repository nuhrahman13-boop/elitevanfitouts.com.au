High-Impact Vehicle Signage, Ute Decals, and Fleet Branding in Dandenong

Your vehicle is a moving billboard and your most powerful marketing asset. Elite Van Fitouts provides elite-tier vehicle signage and fleet branding services designed to turn traffic into high-paying leads. Operating out of our Rowville HQ, our graphic design and vinyl installation teams utilize only premium, automotive-grade Avery Dennison, 3M, and Arlon cast vinyl to ensure your brand stands the test of time, harsh UV weather, and worksite abuse. We absolutely refuse to use cheap polymeric stickers or calendared vinyl that shrinks, cracks, or damages your vehicle's paint. We use premium cast vinyl that is poured as a liquid during manufacturing to perfectly conform to your vehicle's complex curves, deep recesses, and distinct body lines without peeling.

Our signage packages cater to every budget and marketing strategy. We offer Identity packages featuring crisp, precision-cut logo decals, contour-cut vinyl lettering, and spot graphics for front doors and rear tailgates. For the ultimate corporate presence, we provide custom full vehicle wraps, complete color change wraps (matte, gloss, satin, and metallic), vivid digital print graphics, perforated one-way vision window mesh (for rear barn doors, tailgates, and side windows), custom ute tray decals, aluminum canopy signage, half wraps, bonnet protectors, and high-visibility Class 1 reflective tape installations (Chapter 8 compliance) for mine-spec and civil construction vehicles. From a single tradie ute branding project to a massive corporate fleet branding rollout across Melbourne, Elite delivers unmatched visual authority and brand scaling.
