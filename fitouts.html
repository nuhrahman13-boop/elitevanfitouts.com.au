The Best Custom Van Fitouts in Melbourne | Professional Van Racking, Heavy Duty Drawers & Raised Flooring

Elite Van Fitouts is the ultimate destination for premium, highly durable commercial vehicle storage systems and tradie van setups. Located in Rowville, Victoria, we service all trades across Melbourne's Eastern and South Eastern suburbs. Whether you are a commercial electrician needing secure cable reel storage, a licensed plumber requiring heavy-duty tool drawers and pipe storage, or a residential builder needing massive raised false floors for timber, conduit, and ladder storage, we engineer and manufacture the best custom van fitouts in the industry.

Custom Van Shelving, Slide Out Workbenches, and Raised False Floors

Stop wasting billable hours digging through a messy, unorganized van. We maximize your commercial cargo space with precision-machined storage solutions. We design, manufacture, and install ultra-heavy-duty sliding drawers (rated to a massive 227kg dynamic load capacity), raised false floors with hidden under-floor tunnels designed specifically for 3m PVC conduit, copper pipes, and long spirit levels, alongside fully custom plywood shelving units. Every build is cut using advanced computer-guided precision machinery to perfectly scribe to the unique interior body curves and wheel arches of your specific vehicle, ensuring a flawless, rattle-free fit every single time.

Premium Construction Materials: 15mm Hex Plywood, Ply Lining, and Marine Carpet

We do not use cheap, flimsy materials that warp or snap under pressure. Our heavy-duty van setups are constructed using premium 15mm Hex Plywood, which features a highly anti-scratch, water-resistant, and reflective phenolic coating. This ensures your commercial racking can withstand the daily abuse of a harsh construction worksite. We also offer full vehicle interior ply lining, wall paneling, and high-grade charcoal automotive marine carpet to provide essential road sound deadening, thermal insulation, and interior panel protection for your expensive work van.

Trade-Specific Van Setups: Custom Storage Layouts for Every Workflow
