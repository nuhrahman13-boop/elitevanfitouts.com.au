04. Technical Breakdown

False Floor Systems

The foundation of every Elite fitout. We raise the floor level to create massive storage tunnels underneath. This space is perfect for long ladders, spirit levels, and conduit that usually clutter the cabin. You can choose to leave these tunnels open for maximum versatility or fit them with our heavy-duty rear drawers. Top surface options include our premium Hex-Anti Skid ply for grip, or carpet for sound dampening.

Custom Shelving Architecture

We don't do "one size fits all." Our shelving units are CNC machined to fit the exact contours of your van walls, maximizing every inch of width. Whether you run Milwaukee Packout, Festool Systainers, or just generic tubs, we adjust the height and angle of every shelf to match your gear. Tray shelving can be built to any length to accommodate specialized tools or materials.

Tactical Add-Ons

It's the small details that save time. Our Silicone Holders keep adhesive tubes organized and stop them rolling under seats. Door Pockets utilize the empty space on your rear barn doors for paperwork, gloves, and rags. Everything we build is designed to put the tools you use most often right at your fingertips.
