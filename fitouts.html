02. Racking & Components

Custom tailored to your specific tools. No standard sizing.

Crate Shelves

FOR LOOSE PARTS

Angled shelving designed specifically for Euro-Crates. Perfect for plumbers and electricians who carry hundreds of small fittings. Grab the crate, take it to the job, slide it back in. Custom sizes available to fit your existing bins.

Toolbox Shelves

PACKOUT / MAKPAC

Massive open shelves with a high retention lip. Designed to hold heavy system cases like Milwaukee Packout, Makita Makpac, or DeWalt T-Stak. We measure your specific cases and build the shelf to fit them perfectly.

Organiser Shelves

SMALL COMPARTMENTS

Fixed dividers for sprays, glues, and loose tools. Keeps everything upright and easy to grab. We can space the dividers to fit your specific bottles and gear.

Silicone Holder

ADD-ON

Stops tubes rolling around the van. Vertical storage for sealant and adhesive cartridges. Mounts to the end panel of any shelving unit.
