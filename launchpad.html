The Ultimate Business in a Box for Melbourne Tradies | Launchpad by Elite Van Fitouts

Welcome to The Launchpad by Elite Van Fitouts, the industry's first true "Business in a Box" incubation program designed exclusively for Australian tradesmen. Located in Rowville, Victoria, we service ambitious contractors, solo operators, and scaling fleet managers across Melbourne's Eastern and South Eastern suburbs. If you are starting a new plumbing business, launching an electrical contracting company, or scaling a mobile mechanic fleet, we build your entire operational infrastructure simultaneously under one roof.

Simultaneous Production: Van Fitouts, Fleet Branding, and SEO Trade Websites

Stop wasting months trying to coordinate five different vendors. The Launchpad eliminates the friction of scaling. While our precision-machining workshop engineers your heavy-duty custom van fitout and fabricates your raised false floor, our graphic design team is printing and installing your premium Avery Dennison vehicle wrap. Simultaneously, our digital tech department is constructing your high-converting, heavily SEO-optimized trade website. You drop off an empty commercial van and walk out with a fully branded, legally compliant, and digitally automated business ready to dominate the Melbourne market and generate endless inbound leads.

Advanced Tradie CRM and Business Automation

A website without a system is useless. Every Launchpad deployment includes a fully integrated Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system explicitly configured for tradesmen. We build out advanced business automation including Missed Call Text-Back routing (so you never lose a lead to a competitor when you are busy on the tools), automated Google Review generation campaigns to build local trust, unified inbox management (syncing Facebook, Instagram, SMS, and Email into one single app), instant quoting software, calendar booking engines, and database reactivation tools. We systemize your trade so you can focus on billable hours.

Trade-Specific Setups: Custom Layouts for Every Workflow

Your physical and digital infrastructure is 100% customizable to your exact operational requirements. We provide the absolute best professional physical storage setups and digital marketing funnels for: Electricians, Plumbers, Gas Fitters, Air Conditioning Techs (HVAC), Builders, Carpenters, Data and Communications experts, Security Installers, Roofers, Glaziers, Painters, Mobile Dog Grooming Vans, Mobile Coffee Vans, Delivery Couriers, and Civil Contractors.
