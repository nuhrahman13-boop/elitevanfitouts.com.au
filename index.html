The Best Custom Van Fitouts in Melbourne | Racking, Drawers & Flooring

Elite Van Fitouts is the ultimate destination for premium, highly durable commercial vehicle storage systems. Located in Rowville, Victoria, we service all trades across Melbourne's Eastern and South Eastern suburbs. Whether you are an electrician needing cable storage, a plumber needing heavy-duty tool drawers, or a builder needing massive false floors for timber and ladder storage, we engineer the best custom van fitouts in the industry.

Custom Van Shelving, Drawers, and Raised False Floors

Stop wasting time digging through a messy van. We maximize your cargo space with precision-machined storage solutions. We design, manufacture, and install ultra-heavy-duty sliding drawers (rated to 227kg dynamics), raised false floors with hidden under-floor tunnels for 3m PVC conduit and spirit levels, and custom plywood shelving units. Every build is cut using advanced machine precision to perfectly scribe to the unique interior curves of your specific vehicle, ensuring a flawless, rattle-free fit.

Premium Materials: Hex Plywood and Marine Carpet

We do not use cheap materials. Our van setups are constructed using premium 15mm Hex Plywood, which features a highly anti-scratch, water-resistant, and reflective phenolic coating. This ensures your racking can withstand the daily abuse of a harsh worksite. We also offer full vehicle ply lining and high-grade charcoal automotive marine carpet to provide essential sound deadening, insulation, and interior protection for your van.

Trade-Specific Van Setups

A mobile mechanic has completely different workflow needs compared to a locksmith or HVAC technician. Our modular systems are 100% customizable. Add on specific components such as Milwaukee Packout docking stations, Makita Makpac shelves, angled Euro Crate shelving, silicone cartridge holders, spray can dividers, secure gas cylinder ventilation enclosures, 12V dual battery systems, pure sine wave inverters, LED interior strip lighting, and ADR-tested cargo barriers. We provide the best setups for Electricians, Plumbers, Gas Fitters, Air Conditioning Techs, Builders, Carpenters, Data and Communications experts, Security Installers, Roofers, Glaziers, Painters, and Civil Contractors.

Vehicle Compatibility

Our racking systems are perfectly engineered for every major commercial van and ute canopy on the Australian market. We provide fitouts for Toyota HiAce LWB and SLWB, Ford Transit Custom, Ford Ranger Canopies, LDV Deliver 9, LDV G10, Hyundai Staria Load, Mercedes Benz Vito, Mercedes Benz Sprinter (MWB, LWB, High Roof), Volkswagen Transporter T6.1, Volkswagen Crafter, Renault Trafic, Renault Master, Peugeot Trafic, Hyundai iLoad, Mitsubishi Staria, Isuzu D-Max, and Nissan Navara service bodies.

Vehicle Signage and Fleet Branding

Beyond internal storage, Elite Van Fitouts is also Dandenong and Rowville's premier vehicle signage and fleet branding specialist. Turn your van into a 24/7 lead generation machine. We only use premium cast vinyl from Avery Dennison and 3M, guaranteeing your wrap conforms to deep vehicle recesses without shrinking or peeling. We offer computer-cut logo decals, high-visibility partial wraps, full custom vehicle wraps, color changes, digital print graphics, one-way vision window mesh, custom ute tray decals, half wraps, and Class 1 reflective tape installations.
