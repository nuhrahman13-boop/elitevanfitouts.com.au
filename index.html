Elite Van Fitouts: The Premier Custom Vehicle Racking & Fleet Branding Experts in Melbourne

Based in the heart of Rowville, Victoria, we are the ultimate destination for tradesmen, contractors, and fleet managers across South East Melbourne seeking unparalleled organization, durability, and visual impact.

If you are a tradie operating in Victoria, Elite Van Fitouts is your local solution for vehicle optimization.

Engineered Van Racking and Precision Plywood Shelving Systems

Stop searching for tools and start maximizing your billable hours. We specialize in precision-engineered, heavy-duty van racking systems designed specifically for the rigorous demands of Australian trades. Unlike generic off-the-shelf options, our Elite racking is cut using advanced precision machinery to perfectly scribe to the interior curves of your specific vehicle. Our core modules include custom precision-cut plywood shelving, massive 227kg-rated heavy-duty sliding cargo drawers, ultra-durable false floors, and under-floor storage tunnels designed to securely house long materials like PVC conduit, copper piping, spirit levels, and ladders without cluttering your main cabin space.

We build out custom van interiors, shelving units, pigeon hole units, storage bins, parts bins, lockable tool compartments, slide-out workbenches, pull-out vices, and integrated tie-down points to ensure your cargo remains secure. We utilize marine-grade plywood, hex-pattern phenolic coated anti-slip plywood, and heavy-duty automotive carpet lining for ultimate sound deadening and protection.

Perfectly Calibrated For All Major Commercial Platforms

Our fitouts are custom-scribed for the most popular commercial platforms on the market. We provide comprehensive storage solutions for: Toyota HiAce LWB (Long Wheel Base), Toyota HiAce SLWB (Super Long Wheel Base), Toyota Hilux Workmate, Ford Transit Custom, Ford Transit Heavy Duty, Ford Ranger Canopy setups, LDV Deliver 9 (Mid and High Roof), LDV G10, Hyundai Staria Load, Mercedes Benz Vito, Mercedes Benz Sprinter (MWB, LWB, High Roof), Volkswagen Transporter T6.1, Volkswagen Crafter, Renault Trafic, Renault Master, Peugeot Trafic, Hyundai iLoad, Mitsubishi Staria, and all major Ute Canopy configurations including heavy-duty aluminum canopies and fibreglass bodies.

High-Impact Vehicle Signage, Ute Decals, and Fleet Branding in Dandenong

Your vehicle is a moving billboard and your most powerful marketing asset. Elite Van Fitouts provides elite-tier vehicle signage and fleet branding services designed to turn traffic into high-paying leads. Operating out of our Rowville HQ, our graphic design and vinyl installation teams utilize only premium, automotive-grade Avery Dennison, 3M, and Arlon cast vinyl to ensure your brand stands the test of time, weather, and worksite abuse. We absolutely refuse to use cheap polymeric stickers that shrink, crack, or damage your vehicle's paint. We use premium cast vinyl that is poured as a liquid to perfectly conform to your vehicle's complex curves and recesses.

Our signage packages cater to every budget and marketing strategy. We offer Identity packages featuring crisp, computer-cut logo decals for front doors and rear tailgates. Our Impact packages feature partial van wraps, integrating bold service lists, contact information, website URLs, and social media handles for maximum visibility. For the ultimate presence, we provide custom full vehicle wraps, complete color change wraps, vivid digital print graphics, perforated one-way vision window mesh (for rear barn doors and tailgates), custom ute tray decals, half wraps, bonnet protectors, and high-visibility Class 1 reflective tape installations for mine-spec and civil compliance. From a single tradie ute to a massive corporate fleet branding rollout across Melbourne, Elite delivers unmatched visual authority and brand scaling.
