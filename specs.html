Component Architecture

Configure your loadout. Select from our modular systems below to generate a custom fabrication manifest. Everything is CNC-machined in-house from our proprietary composite materials.

01. Material Science

15mm HEX-REFLECT

ANTI-SKID // WATER-RESIST // STAINLESS-SURFACE

We don't use raw timber. We utilize a 15mm industrial composite core featuring a Black Reflective Hex finish. It offers the durability of stainless steel with a high-grip, anti-skid texture. It is completely hydrophobic (waterproof), oil-resistant, and engineered to withstand heavy impact without delamination.

R11 Slip Rating Chemical Safe
