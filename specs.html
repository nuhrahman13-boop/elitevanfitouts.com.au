Component Architecture

Build your own loadout. Select the specific components you require below to generate a custom manifest. We do not use chipboard or standard runners. All materials are industrial grade.

01. Structural Foundation

False Floor System

MATERIAL: HEX-PHENOLIC BIRCH PLY

The foundation of every Elite build. CNC-machined from 12mm Birch Core Plywood with a Phenolic Hex-Pattern face. This material is hydrophobic (water-repellant), oil-resistant, and provides R10 slip resistance.

THICKNESS12mm
SURFACEAnti-Skid Hex
MOISTUREHydrophobic

Heavy Duty Drawers

HARDWARE: 227KG LOCKING SLIDES

Gravity is the enemy. Standard runners fail on inclines. We utilize 227kg-rated locking slides. A trigger mechanism locks the drawer closed for transit, and locks it open for safety while working.

LOAD RATING227kg (Dynamic)
LOCK TYPEIn / Out Lock
DEPTH900mm - 1500mm

02. Storage Modules

Skeleton Shelving

DESIGN: MORTISE & TENON

Weight equates to fuel consumption. Our "Skeleton" design removes 40% of material weight without compromising rigidity. Shelves are bonded and screwed for a rattle-free existence.

MATERIAL12mm Ply
JOINERYCNC Interlock
CONFIGCustom Height
